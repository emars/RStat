RStat

Reddit Link Statistics
About

This simple chrome extension allows you to keep track of how much reddit you've been browsing. Simply download the extension and login to you reddit account. At any time, click the extension to view how many links you've browsed in total.

Navigate to rstat.emaf.ca/me and log in using your reddit credentials to see detailed graphs and statistics about your redditing.

You'll get a simple UI along with graphs to visualize your browsing habits.
