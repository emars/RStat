RStat

Reddit Link Statistics
About

This simple chrome extension allows you to visualize your reddit browsing habits. Simply download the extension and make sure you're logged in to your reddit account.

At any time, navigate to rstat.emaf.ca/me and log in using your reddit credentials to see detailed graphs about your browsing.

Note, RStat is currently in Beta. Feel free to post issues on the github page for this project or send me a pm to _emars.
